Beauty could be the myth, for that value intuition is ignorance: it is what that is not language and hence non-linguistical, i.e. not rigourous, yet from the monothestic perspective of Christianity it must have been one and identical with the mysterious beauty-itself, God so to speak because this is that intuition from which God personifies: the desire glorified into a beauty, externalized, and as an external, unsolvable myth with such moral implication of absolute freedom, personified into such ignorance-bounding ethical singularity.
The intuition to value, or beauty, is an intuition alluding to the absolute meaning and hence repels being identified into desires, i.e. set into the respective contexts of desires since absoluteness requires absolute (from here mythical) positions of unquestionable statics akin to that of the bio-logical inertias that defines organ-isms: desires strives to bound the hyperlinguistical for a stable supply of ignorance, the hyperlinguistical most likely sourced as forgetfulness.

The act of "doing something" is an expression to the desire for meaningful achievements, in particular here as repression of meaning-censorship, hence necessarily greedy since one has to be limited in sightfulness as to avoid any abstraction, any attempt into higher beauties and better taste to avoid falling yet again into the ultimate, never failing meaning-censorship that waits for us in all ends. The censorship, albeit usually sufficient in its moral form, also works in an analytical method when the yearning for meaning take a step down to enclose itself in a sense of "private meaning", sarcastically denoting itself as romantic, yet it could be intuitively shown that the romanticism is one and the same as the private meaning-itself.
It is beyond the liguistical capability to desire for happiness.

就文化而言，财产被规定为自由（意志得实现的潜能）分配的介质直观，而当储蓄本身又被认定为一种奖励，就受游戏模式的污染、成为积分，即自明为（一种）根本价值的直观，远远超越了其原本作为意志实现之可能性（原本、在伦理上，作为各个自由意志在一维时间中轮均实现的分配机制）：在积分意象的冲击下财产（其完整说法应为财产分配）的这功能已经在政治上残疾了，只能将目标定于财产的积存本身之上，才不至丧失去专注而赋义于财产所及的标价范围内任何之事的能力……
奖励鸡腿和奖励钱是两码事，因为前者中仍暗含着对鸡腿厨子的奖励（因为若消费仍是对自由的公平再分配，自由的潜能将不断被交换到下一人手中），储蓄行为使这一机制停滞，而当人们不得不为数不过来的零重新赋以自由潜能再分配之外的意义，经济秩序就崩溃了。
以物易物总将向更方便的形态演化，储蓄或生于这逻辑，其超出实用范围的膨胀却不止于此（实用的／“经济的”）逻辑，在对根本价值的渴望史中，它自造为价值系统，以至于要说：钱不是什么都能办到的（这时我们已经忘了，我们的许多愿望不是全社会齐心协力就有实现的。）

因为整个场都因场的动态而显现，对语言提出新的维度要求，语言则总处于遗忘中，它的书写总不及它自身
因为现书写下的已经被如此翻译了，而这翻译所失的十之八九（因为书写本身就阻滞思维，越是不直观的就越难以被译出）本就是难称语言、不能阅读的
所恐惧的总是词的不可逾越性，回溯至疯狂的概念：被代以疯狂这一词禁忌的本是对自身的私人愚蠢的一般体验，问题在一般化上：这要求打通作为常人-疯子间的区隔的疯狂概念，最终将疯狂还原为根本上是语言关系中的一种单方面看法，也即逻辑间距本身：词语各个的显现；所谓“词的不可逾越性”也指明为词的结构，“不可逾越”辨证地暗示了统合的潜能。清醒即可读性，多义的语言也可在不同意义上不同地清醒，且相互保持疯狂。
政治对立总向其数目回归，所有二元对立也向二元对立的抽象回归一处。
Clarity is clarity in mentality and language... still the structures, although made more accurate by limiting itself to the domain of philosophical linguistics, manifests imprecision because every word is so tainted that to speak is to mistranslate.
